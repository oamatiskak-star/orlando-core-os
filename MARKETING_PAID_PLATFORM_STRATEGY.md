## 1. POSITIONERING & BETAALD PLATFORM

### 1.1 Platform-vorm: **Hybrid Membership + Skool Community**

**Keuze:** Geen apart betaald LMS; in plaats daarvan:
- **YouTube Channel Memberships** (laag entry €4,99–€9,99/mnd) voor exclusieve video's + Discord
- **Skool Community** (Pro plan €99/mnd) als premiumlaag: gesloten leercommunity + handcuratie + live Q&A per kanaal

**Voordelen:**
- YouTube memberships = lage friction, al in YouTube UI
- Skool = structuur voor betaalde kurussen later (Finance 101, Vastgoed Beginners)
- Geen extra betaalinfra; Skool + YouTube beide native payment

### 1.2 Tier-structuur (per kanaal gelijk)

| Tier | Prijs | Inhoud | Target |
|------|-------|--------|--------|
| **YouTube Member** | €4,99/mnd | Exclusieve weekly video, badge, Discord acces | 100–200 members/kanaal |
| **Skool Pro** | €99/mnd | Alles + live Q&A, cursus, handcuratie, signalen | 10–20 members/kanaal |

**Realistisch revenue per kanaal (maand 1–3):**
- YouTube memberships: 20–30 members × €4,99 = €100–150
- Skool Pro: 2–5 members × €99 = €200–500
- **Per kanaal subtotaal:** €300–650/mnd
- **8 kanalen:** €2.4k–5.2k/mnd (na 3 mnd growth)

## 2. 3-LAGEN MONETISATIEMODEL

### 2.1 Funnel-architectuur (geen kanibalisering)

```
VIEW (YouTube video)
  ↓
  ├─ ADSENSE (passief, alle viewers, ~2% RPM NL/EN finance)
  ├─ AFFILIATE LINK (in beschrijving, 15–25% click-through video)
  └─ LEAD MAGNET (e-mail signup, 3–5% van viewers)
       ↓
       EMAIL NURTURE (7-email sequence, 14 dagen)
       ├─ Affiliate deep-dives (product reviews)
       ├─ YouTube membership pitch (€4,99)
       └─ Skool intro (€99, "kursusopeningskorting" €49 eerste maand)
            ↓
            PAID TIERS
            ├─ YouTube Member (gesloten vids, Discord)
            └─ Skool Pro (cursus, live)
```

**Kanibalisering voorkomen:**
- AdSense: alle viewers (zero friction)
- Affiliate: geen upsell, pure informatief (product comparison video → "hier kun je dit kopen")
- Membership: alleen engaged viewers (>80% watch time) zien pitch in outro
- Skool: only voor YouTube members (gating, verhoogde commitment)

### 2.2 Per-laag conversion targets

| Laag | Audience | Conv.% | Waarde/User |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|
| AdSense | 100% views | 0.5% | €0,20 (estimated RPM) |
| Affiliate | 25% (linkers) | 2% | €5–€20 (EPC * commission) |
| YouTube Member | 5% (pitch) | 8% | €4,99/mnd |
| Skool Pro | 1% (from members) | 15% | €99/mnd |

**Scenario 1000 views/week:**
- AdSense: €2–4/week
- Affiliate: 250 × 2% = 5 clicks/week × €10 avg = €50/week
- YouTube Member: 50 × 8% = 4 signups/week × €4,99 = €20/week
- Skool: 4 × 15% = 0,6 conversies × €99 = €59/week
- **Totaal:** €131–159/week = €524–636/mnd per 1000 weekly views

## 4. FUNNEL: YOUTUBE → EMAIL → COMMUNITY → PAID

### 4.1 Video-CTA strategie

**In-video struktur (alle kanalen gelijk):**

- **00:00–30s:** Hook + affiliate preview ("Vandaag review ik Binance affiliate partner…")
- **Middle:** Core content + affiliate comparison table
- **Last 60s:** 
  - "Klik link in beschrijving voor [Binance/TradingView/Fundrise] → directe signup met mijn affiliate link"
  - "Abonneer + bel het bellencje voor exclusive member-only reviews"
  - "Link naar community: [Skool community link]"

**Click-through design:**
- Affiliate link = top beschrijving (opvallend, met badge "exclusive partner")
- YouTube Membership = mid-beschrijving ("join my community")
- Skool Community = low-friction CTA ("try free 7 days")

### 4.2 Email-sequentie (7 emails, 14 dagen)

**Lead magnet:** "Top 5 Finance Affiliate Programs [niche] — Affiliate Disclosure Included" (PDF)

**Sequentie:**
1. **Email 1 (dag 0):** Welkom + affiliate disclosure
2. **Email 2 (dag 2):** "3 mistakes I made with [Binance/TradingView]" + affiliate link
3. **Email 3 (dag 4):** Comparison table: program A vs B (affiliate links)
4. **Email 4 (dag 6):** Case study: "Earned €500/month with [program]" (affiliate link)
5. **Email 5 (dag 8):** YouTube membership pitch: "Join 200+ members"
6. **Email 6 (dag 10):** Skool community pitch + launch discount code (€99 → €49 first month)
7. **Email 7 (dag 12):** "Last chance: Community closes in 48h" + all links

**Tool:** Bestaande Supabase `user_memberships` + email automation (Resend of Supabase edge functions)

### 4.3 Community funnel (YouTube → Skool)

**Gating strategie:**
- YouTube memberships = direct access Discord (via bot link in description)
- Skool Pro = gated to YouTube members only (verification via email match)

**Skool landing page:** Per kanaal (VastgoedTv, VermogenTv, LoopForge AI, etc.)
- Kopie: "7-daagse masterclass + live Q&A met [kanaalnaam]"
- Social proof: "200+ members, 4.8/5 rating"
- Pricing: €99/mnd, "€49 korting eerste maand"

## 9. AANNAMES & BESLISSINGEN VOOR ORLANDO

### ✅ BEVESTIGD (logica)

1. **3-lagen model werkt zonder kanibalisering** — AdSense (alle viewers) → Affiliate (25%) → Membership (5%) → Skool (1%)
2. **Skool Pro is betere keuze dan YouTube Memberships alleen** — Schaalbare cursusinhoud, hogere LTV (€300 vs €60)
3. **Finance (Binance + TradingView) = snelste path to €3k affiliate MRR** — Hoog EPC, engaged audience, bestaande affiliate infrastructure
4. **Email nurture = critical path** — 3–5% lead → paid conversion = basis funnel velocity

### ❓ BESLISSING NODIG: ORLANDO

1. **Skool licentie kiezen?**
   - Optie A: **Skool Pro (€99/mnd)** — alle kanalen under één account, centraal beheer
   - Optie B: **Skool Hobby (€9/mnd) per kanaal** — meer isolatie, maar fragmented
   - **Aanbeveling:** Optie A (Pro), start met 2 kanalen (juni), schaal naar 8 tegen november

2. **Affiliate payout cadence?**
   - Weekly (Stripe → Moneybird), of Maandelijks (efficiency)?
   - **Aanbeveling:** Maandelijks (accounting overhead ↓, Moneybird batching beter)

   - Optie A: Nieuwe weekly exclusive video (€4,99 members only)
   - Optie B: Gesloten back-catalog (goedkoper, minder workload)
   - **Aanbeveling:** Optie A (week 1–4 archive, daarna mix 60/40 public/exclusive)

4. **Paid ads budget: startgewicht?**
   - €0 (organic only), of €500/mnd (Facebook/Instagram)?
   - **Aanbeveling:** Start €0 juni–juli (organic baseline), scale naar €500 augustus (proven funnel)

5. **Welke email platform?**
   - Resend (developer, gratis, maar zelf automatie bouwen), of Mailchimp/ConvertKit (managed)?
